Aiwass: The Sacred Texts Discord Bot

Aiwass Bot is a powerful tool that allows users to access and explore various sacred texts directly within their Discord server. Whether you're looking to study spiritual writings or integrate sacred passages into discussions, this bot makes it easy and convenient.

Features

  • Fetch Specific Verses: Retrieve specific verses from a chosen book, language, chapter, and verse.
  • Random Verse: Get a random verse from a specified book and language.
  • Search Verses: Search for specific keywords within a book and get all relevant verses.
  • List Available Books: Display information about all available books in the database.

This bot is ideal for spiritual communities, educational groups, or anyone interested in exploring sacred texts. With simple commands and a comprehensive database, the Sacred Texts Discord Bot brings ancient wisdom directly to your Discord server.
